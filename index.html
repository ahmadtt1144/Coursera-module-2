Chicken

Chicken is a widely consumed meat that is both versatile and healthy. It is rich in protein and can be prepared in numerous ways, from grilling and roasting to frying and baking. Popular chicken dishes include chicken curry, grilled chicken breast, and chicken soup.

Beef

Beef is a popular red meat that comes from cattle. It is a rich source of protein, iron, and essential nutrients. Beef can be cooked in a variety of ways, including grilling, stewing, and roasting. Common beef dishes include steak, beef stew, and hamburgers.

Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish that typically consists of vinegared rice, raw fish, and vegetables. It is known for its delicate flavor and artistic presentation. Common types of sushi include nigiri (slices of fish on rice), maki (rolls), and sashimi (slices of raw fish without rice).
